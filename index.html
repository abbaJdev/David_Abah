Fufu

This dish is a staple in West and Central Africa, and also a favorite in the Caribbean. The finished fufu product can be served with soup, stew, or sauce. It is so easy to make, and can be on your table in about 15 minutes!

Jollof Rice

A one-pot African dish, Jollof rice is rich and incredibly tasty! Made from scratch with rice, tomatoes, pimento peppers, tomato paste, scotch bonnet, onions, and spices. It is one of the most popular Nigerian dishes.

Waakye

Rice and beans are a staple in many countries, as it is in Ghana. Here’s the catch, Waakye (pronounced waa-che’) is a popular breakfast dish, which means rice and beans for breakfast!

Egusi Soup

African Chicken Stew

African Chicken Stew is easy to make, aromatic, and flavorful. With a rich, tomato-based sauce, this stew is the perfect weeknight meal. Pair it with some warm plantains and you will have your family’s new favorite chicken dinner!

Gari

Ready for a journey to Nigeria? Then try out this classic West African recipe. Soaked Garri is definitely a must-try for anyone wanting to visit Nigeria for the first time. Made with pan-fried cassava flakes, you can enjoy Garri in many different ways.

  • Salt
